Instructions:


This program can be used to define tessellations of simple origami designs and to visualize its characteristics.


1. You can specify the dimensions of the strip on the left, followed by the alignment of the lines. You can change the colors of each segment by clicking on them, or by using the built-in configurations.


2. The profile of the folded state is displayed on the very right. It is carachterized and approximated by the red curve in the middle.


3. You can create a tessellation from the strip you defined by giving the amount of strip repeats Once ready, you can download the SVG and open it in the simulation.
